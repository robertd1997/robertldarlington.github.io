About me

Hello, my name is Robert Darlington and I am a graduate of the University of Wisconsin – Madison. I graduated in Cartography, GIS, and Geography while working professionally in the UW Cart Lab. I now current work for the City of Orlando as a Cartographer in Flordia. My passions include cartography, photography, filmmaking, outdoor activities, traveling and music.
